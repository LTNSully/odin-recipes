Asian Coleslaw

Description

This asian spin on a classic coleslaw uses sesame and asian flavour.
The result is a bright and fresh slaw that can be used as a side or salad.

Ingredients (quantities up to you)

  • Red Cabbage
  • Green Cabbage/li>
  • Carrots
  • Parsley and/or Cilantro
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Sesame Oil
  • Salt + Pepper
  • Rice Wine Vinegar
  • Green Onions
  • (optional) Chili Oil

Preparation

  • Thinly slice the cabbage
  • Julienne the carrots
  • Chop the herbs

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients into a large bowl
  2. Regrigerate for 30 before serving
